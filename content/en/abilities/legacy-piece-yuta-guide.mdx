<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Yuta Quest Walkthrough">

Once you have met the prerequisites, the actual questline consists of three distinct phases. This walkthrough ensures you don't miss any critical dialogue triggers that could reset your progress.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Initial Contract">
    Speak to Yuta at the Frozen Overlook. Hand over the 50,000 Beli and the 5 Spirit Shards. He will task you with "Cleansing the Spirits" of the island.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Yeti Extermination">
    Defeat 25 **Elite Yetis** (Level 325) scattered around the plateau. These enemies have high health and can stun you with ground slams. Use ranged attacks if possible.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Mirror Match">
    Return to Yuta. He will teleport you to a private arena where you must fight a **Shadow Clone** of yourself. The clone uses your exact current loadout and abilities.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Final Attunement">
    After defeating your clone, speak to Yuta one last time. He will perform a ritual that permanently unlocks the **Mimicry** slot in your ability menu.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Combat Strategy">
During the Mirror Match in Step 3, the AI clone is aggressive but predictable. Unequip your most powerful weapon right before the fight starts, then re-equip it once the clone spawns. The clone will be stuck with the weaker gear!
</Callout>

</Section>
