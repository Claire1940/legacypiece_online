To succeed in your pirate adventure, you must understand how these three systems interact:

1. **Devil Fruit Abilities**: High-impact elemental and physical powers.
2. **Sword Skills**: High-velocity melee strikes and weapon techniques.
3. **Combat Skills**: Universal fighting styles that offer reliable combos and utility.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Sword and Combat Fighting Styles">

If you prefer a weapon-based approach or want to preserve your ability to swim freely, focusing on Sword Skills and Martial Combat Styles is the ideal route. These abilities do not suffer from water penalties and provide incredible single-target physical damage.

### Melee Combat Options

- **Sword Abilities**: Focuses on precise slashing techniques, perfect for breaking enemy guards and dealing burst damage to bosses.
- **Combat Skills**: Hand-to-hand fighting styles that prioritize rapid-fire strikes, knockbacks, and defensive parries to keep enemies at bay.

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Ability Progression Route">

Unlocking and mastering your desired **legacy piece abilities** requires a structured progression path. Follow these steps to maximize your combat efficiency from a fresh character to a late-game powerhouse.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Character Creation & Basics">
    Customize your character and complete the tutorial quests. Focus on learning basic combat mechanics like dodging, blocking, and light attack chains.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Acquire Your First Combat Style">
    Visit early-game trainers to purchase your first basic Sword Style or Fighting Style. This provides your first active skills that do not rely on rare drops.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Hunt for Devil Fruits">
    Search under trees, check spawns, or save in-game currency to obtain a starter Devil Fruit (such as Ice or Fire) to drastically boost your farming speed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Optimize Attribute Points">
    Allocate your stat points to match your primary damage source. If using a fruit, dump points into Fruit Mastery; if using swords, prioritize Sword Mastery.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Challenging High-Level Bosses">
    Combine your fully unlocked abilities to challenge bosses. Use crowd control skills to stun them, followed by your highest damage-dealing melee or elemental attacks.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Resetting Stats">
If you decide to switch from a Sword build to a Fruit build, look out for active promotional codes or in-game NPCs that offer stat resets to avoid ruining your build efficiency.
</Callout>

</Section>
