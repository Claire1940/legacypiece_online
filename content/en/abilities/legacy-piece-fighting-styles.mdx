### Advanced Quest-Based Specs

For the most powerful combat styles, you must complete dedicated questlines rather than simply trading materials.

#### Destructive Moon (Akaza Spec)
Located at the **Slayer Mansion**, this style requires you to complete a multi-stage questline:
- **Muzan Blood**: Obtain and use Muzan Blood multiple times (dropped by the Akaza boss or rolled via the summon banner).
- **Juggling Balls**: Collect 3 Juggling Balls (dropped by the Akaza boss, though these can also be traded from other players).
- **Find Koyuki**: Use the NPC track tab to locate Koyuki and complete the style ritual.

#### One-Eyed Ghoul (Kaneki Spec)
Located at the **Tokyo Ghoul Island**, this spec relies heavily on your character's race:
- **Ghoul Race**: You must talk to the island NPC and hand over specific materials (such as flesh dropped by local mobs) to transform into a Ghoul first.
- **Kaneki Boss**: Defeat the Kaneki boss to gather all required spec drops.
- **Crimson Owl**: Optionally defeat the Crimson Owl world boss for extra high-tier drops.

<Callout type="warning" title="Race Lock Warning">
Do not attempt to farm the materials for Turbo Spirit or One-Eyed Ghoul if you do not have the Yokai or Ghoul races active. The quest NPCs will refuse to interact with you, wasting your hard-earned boss drops.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Guide: Unlocking Hero Hunter">

The **Hero Hunter** (Garou) spec is the most sought-after combat style for competitive players. Unlocking it is a true test of skill, requiring a pure human character and extensive combat victories.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Prepare Your Character">
    Ensure your character is set to the **Human race**. Travel to **A City Island** and locate the Garou boss NPC. You must gather the initial summoning items from the Garou boss (which spawns based on server time or can be manually summoned) to officially begin the questline.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Defeat Flashy Flash">
    The first quest objective requires you to locate and defeat the **Flashy Flash** boss **5 times**. You must land the final blow using standard combat/melee moves for the progress to count.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Conquer Garou">
    The second trial demands that you defeat the **Garou** boss **10 times**. Just like the previous step, these victories must be achieved using close-quarters combat rather than relying on ranged weapons or fruit abilities.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Prove Your Worth in PvP">
    The final and hardest step requires you to **defeat 15 players** in active combat. Head to high-traffic zones or arenas to secure these combat defeats. Once completed, return to the quest giver to permanently unlock the Hero Hunter style.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="PvP Grinding Tip">
To complete the 15-player combat requirement quickly, team up with friends or find willing players in the community Discord. Trading defeats in low-level areas can save you hours of searching for active targets.
</Callout>

</Section>
