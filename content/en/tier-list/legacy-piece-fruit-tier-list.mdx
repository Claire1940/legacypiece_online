### S-Tier Fruits
Fruits in this tier offer the best performance across all game modes. Light provides unmatched utility with its fast travel speed, allowing players to fly between islands without relying on boats. Its skills feature quick cooldowns and long-range targeting, keeping you safe from melee-focused enemies. Dragon, on the other hand, turns the user into a powerhouse of destruction. With high defensive scaling and massive AoE attacks, it is the ultimate choice for players looking to dominate end-game bosses.

### A-Tier Fruits
A-Tier fruits are incredibly strong and can easily carry you through the entire game. Magma is a favorite for PvE grinding due to its high burn damage that melts boss health pools. Quake focuses on raw force, offering high knockback and crowd control capabilities. Mochi stands out as a highly versatile option, blending physical melee strikes with elemental properties to create long, inescapable skill combos in PvP fights.

### B-Tier Fruits
These fruits are balanced and highly accessible. Ice and Fire are excellent starting options for players who need reliable elemental damage to level up. Ice offers freeze mechanics that lock enemies in place, making combat straightforward for beginners. Dark provides utility with gravity-based pulls and dark clouds, making it a solid choice for hybrid sword builds.
