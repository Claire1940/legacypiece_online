### Strategic Breakdowns

- **Mochi**: This fruit is a favorite among PvP enthusiasts. Its ability to lock down targets allows you to transition smoothly into sword or fighting style combos.
- **Quake**: If you prefer raw power, Quake provides massive shockwaves that clear entire camps of enemies. It is highly effective for defending areas.
- **Magma**: Magma pools deal continuous damage to any enemy standing inside them. This makes it the absolute best choice for stationary boss fights.

<Callout type="warning" title="Warning">
While Magma deals incredible damage, it lacks high-speed mobility. You must invest in solid dodging techniques to avoid getting zoned out by ranged PvP players.
</Callout>
