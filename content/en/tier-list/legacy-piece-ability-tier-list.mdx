<Section icon={Swords} title="A & B-Tier Abilities: Strong & Balanced Options">

A-Tier and B-Tier abilities are highly viable and can easily carry you through all content. While they might lack the extreme mobility of Light or the raw AoE of Dragon, they offer unique utility and highly competitive damage output.

### A-Tier: High-Performance Powers
These abilities are specialized tools that excel when built correctly. **Mochi** is a combo powerhouse in PvP, while **Magma** offers the highest raw damage-over-time for melting high-health bosses.

### B-Tier: Reliable Progression Tools
Perfect for mid-game progression. **Ice** allows you to freeze enemies in place, making it incredibly safe for solo players. **Fire** provides a highly balanced, easy-to-learn moveset with decent burn damage.

<Section icon={Target} title="How to Unlock and Upgrade Abilities">

Unlocking the top-tier powers in Legacy Piece requires a mix of exploration, boss farming, and luck. Follow these steps to build your character efficiently.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire a Power Source">
    Search for Devil Fruits spawning under trees, purchase them from the fruit dealer, or win them from high-level boss drops.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Train Your Mastery">
    Equip your chosen ability and defeat enemies near your level. Using the skills repeatedly increases your mastery level, unlocking stronger moves.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Incorporate Sword Skills">
    Visit weapon trainers across the islands to learn sword abilities. Combining physical sword strikes with fruit powers creates the most optimal combat builds.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Optimize Stat Points">
    Allocate your stat points to match your build. If using fruits, dump points into Fruit Mastery. If using swords, prioritize Strength and Sword Mastery.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Build Synergy">
Do not neglect your basic fighting styles. Even an S-Tier fruit user needs a solid physical backup skill when their primary abilities are on cooldown.
</Callout>
