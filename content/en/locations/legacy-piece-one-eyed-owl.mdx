<Section icon={Shield} title="Combat Strategies & Boss Mechanics">

Conquering the Crimson Owl requires a strategic approach. The boss features a massive health pool and utilizes powerful, wide-sweeping melee attacks alongside destructive ranged projectiles that can easily wipe unprepared groups.

| Attack Name | Move Description | Optimal Counter Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Kagune Sweep** | Large horizontal melee strike | Double-jump or dash backward to evade |
| **Rc Projectile Rain** | Ranged AoE barrage | Keep moving in a circular pattern |
| **Frenzy Charge** | Fast forward dash attack | Sidestep just before the boss charges |

To survive the encounter, players should focus on a hybrid build that balances high mobility with steady damage output. Utilizing ranged abilities, such as the Storm Esper style, allows you to chip away at the boss's health from a safe distance while keeping an eye out for telegraph animations. When the boss prepares its Frenzy Charge, immediately dash laterally to avoid taking massive collision damage.

<Callout type="tip" title="Build Suggestion">
Pairing a high-defense accessory set with health-regen food is highly recommended. If you are playing solo, focus on keeping your distance and utilizing hit-and-run tactics rather than engaging in sustained melee combat.
</Callout>
