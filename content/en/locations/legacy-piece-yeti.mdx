<Section icon={Map} title="Yeti Boss Location & Requirements">

To challenge the **Legacy Piece Yeti**, you must travel to Shimotsuki Island, which is balanced for players between levels 40 and 50. Unlike other bosses that patrol open fields, the Yeti is hidden away in a subterranean cavern. Finding him requires navigating a short questline and locating a secret entrance.

| Island | Level Range | Boss Name | Spawn Location | Boss HP |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Shimotsuki Island | Levels 40–50 | Yeti | Secret Deserted Lab Cave | 750 HP |

<Callout type="warning" title="Level Warning">

</Callout>

To access the Yeti's cave, you need to follow these specific steps:

  <Step num="1" title="Find the Scientist">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Find the Trapdoor">
    Search the floor of the deserted lab to find a hidden trapdoor.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Enter the Ice Cave">
    Open the trapdoor and drop down into the frozen cave where the Yeti waits.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics & Movesets">

The Yeti boss possesses 750 HP and deals damage scaling from 9 to 20 per hit. He utilizes three signature area-of-effect (AoE) abilities that can easily overwhelm unprepared adventurers.

| Ability Name | Attack Type | Effect & Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Yeti Roar** | AoE Shockwave | Emits a loud roar that stuns and knocks back players. |
| **Ceiling Bust** | Falling Hazards | Strikes the cavern roof to rain down sharp, damaging icicles. |
| **Ground Slam** | Heavy Melee | Slams the frozen ground, dealing high AoE damage around him. |
