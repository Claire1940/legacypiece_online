Once you reach the **legacy piece zenitsu location** and meet the rank prerequisites, the NPC will issue a multi-stage questline. Completing these steps is mandatory to finalize the weapon purchase.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initiate the Slayer Trial">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Enter the Infinity Castle">
    Demons can only be hunted within the **Infinity Castle** dungeon. To access this area, wait until nighttime and locate the **Muzan NPC** spawning around the mansion grounds. Interact with Muzan to teleport to the dungeon.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Survive the Dungeon Waves">

  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Finalize and Purchase">
    Return to Zenitsu at the Slayer Mansion with your completed quest log. Hand over **$1,000,000 Beli** to officially claim your Mythic Zenitsu weapon.
  </Step>
</Steps>
