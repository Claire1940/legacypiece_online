<Section icon={Swords} title="Boss Abilities & Combat Mechanics">

Don Krieg utilizes a variety of ranged and melee attacks that can quickly drain your health bar. His moveset is designed to punish players who stay stationary for too long. Recognizing his visual cues is key to dodging his most devastating abilities.

| Ability Name | Attack Type | Damage Range | Mitigation Strategy |
|---|---|---|---|
| MH5 Poison Gas | AoE (Area of Effect) | High DoT | Dash backward immediately |
| Explosive Shot | Ranged Projectile | Medium | Block or dodge laterally |
| Spear Thrust | Melee Charge | Medium | Perfect block to stun |
| Iron Shield Block | Defensive | N/A | Use guard-break skills |

The most dangerous attack is the MH5 Poison Gas. When Don Krieg raises his launcher, he will fire a purple canister that explodes into a wide toxic cloud. Standing in this cloud applies a stackable poison debuff that bypasses standard defense ratings.

<Callout type="warning" title="Poison Hazard">
The poison gas cloud lingers for 8 seconds. Never attempt to melee the boss while the gas cloud is active on top of him.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Solo Strategy">

Defeating the boss solo requires patience and precise execution. By following a structured approach, you can minimize damage taken and consistently defeat the boss without relying on a group.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Clear the Deck">
    Eliminate the three patrolling Krieg Pirates on the deck before engaging the boss to prevent getting swarmed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Bait the Poison Gas">
    Engage the boss from a distance to force him to use his MH5 Poison Gas early in the fight.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execute Guard Breaks">
    When the boss uses his Iron Shield, perform a heavy attack or a guard-breaking skill to stun him.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Punish and Retreat">
    Land your main combo while he is stunned, then quickly dash away to avoid his counter-attacks.
  </Step>
</Steps>
