<Section icon={Map} title="Map Guide: Finding the Legacy Piece Cid Location">

If you want to optimize your character's build in Roblox, finding the **legacy piece cid location** is one of the first things you need to do. Cid is the dedicated Stat Resetter NPC in Legacy Piece. Because players often change their minds about their playstyle—moving from a Swordsman build to a Devil Fruit user—knowing how to navigate back to this NPC is essential for progression.

Cid is positioned on **Foosha Island**, the very first island where all new players spawn. This makes him highly accessible for low-level players, though high-level players will need to sail back to the starter zone to utilize his services. 

To find Cid, head to the main village area on Foosha Island. Look for the large tavern building near the harbor. Walk directly behind the tavern, towards the rocky cliffs and the shoreline. Cid stands in the shaded area behind the building, wearing simple clothing. He does not have a giant neon sign above his head, so look closely near the back walls of the tavern structure.

| Feature | Detail |
| :--- | :--- |
| **NPC Name** | Cid (Stat Resetter) |
| **Island Name** | Foosha Island (Starter Island) |
| **Landmark** | Behind the main village tavern, near the docks |
| **Spawn Level** | Level 1 to 15 zone |
| **NPC Function** | Resets all allocated stat points to zero |

<Callout type="tip" title="Navigation Tip">

<Section icon={Swords} title="Stat Reset Costs and Requirements">

Before you make the journey to the **legacy piece cid location**, you must ensure you have gathered the necessary resources. Cid does not perform his services for free. Every time you request a stat reset, you must pay a flat rate of Beli, the primary in-game currency.

Currently, Cid charges **20,000 Beli** for a single stat reset. For early-game players, this amount can feel substantial, but it becomes trivial as you progress to higher-level islands. If you lack the funds, you will need to farm local quests on Foosha Island or defeat nearby enemies to accumulate the cash.

| Requirement | Details |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Minimum Level** | None (Available to all levels) |
| **Beli Cost** | 20,000 Beli per reset |
| **Robux Alternative** | 50 Robux via the in-game shop |
| **Reset Limits** | Unlimited (As long as you can pay the Beli fee) |
| **Retained Attributes** | Your level, inventory, and Devil Fruits are completely safe |

<Callout type="warning" title="Important Warning">
Resetting your stats only refunds your attribute points (Melee, Defense, Sword, Devil Fruit, Stamina). It does **not** delete your equipped Devil Fruit, nor does it reset your level or mastery progress. You will keep your current fruit capacity and skills.
</Callout>

To farm the 20,000 Beli quickly on Foosha Island:
- Accept quests from the Mayor to defeat local Bandits.
- Defeat the Bandit Boss, who spawns near the forest area of the island.
- Sell any unwanted starter weapons or items to local merchants.
