To maximize your efficiency, follow this tactical progression during any major encounter:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Pattern Recognition">
    Observe the boss for the first 30 seconds. Most bosses follow a 3-hit combo followed by a heavy AOE (Area of Effect) skill. Learn the visual cues for these heavy hits.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Ability Chaining">
    Start with your ranged Fruit abilities (like Light's beams) to chip away at their shield. Once the shield is broken, move in with Sword abilities for massive burst damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Stamina Management">
    Never let your stamina bar hit zero. Always keep enough for at least two dodges. If you are low, back off and use the environment for cover while it recharges.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Execution Phase">
    When the boss reaches 20% health, they will often enter a desperate state. Save your Ultimate ability for this moment to end the fight quickly before they can heal or call for reinforcements.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Fruit Synergy">
Using the **Light Fruit** is highly recommended for boss farming due to its high mobility, allowing you to stay out of range of devastating melee combos while dealing consistent damage.
</Callout>
