<Section icon={Swords} title="Weapon Systems & Combat Mechanics">

In the vast open world of the Roblox pirate RPG, mastering **legacy piece weapons** is crucial for survival and progression. Unlike basic punch combat, weapons offer dedicated skill trees, unique movesets, and scaling attributes that significantly boost your damage output. Players can choose to specialize entirely in swordplay or combine weapon skills with powerful Devil Fruit abilities to create versatile hybrid builds.

Understanding how weapons interact with your character stats is the first step toward build optimization. Most weapons scale directly with your Sword or Strength attributes. Investing points into these stats unlocks additional skills, increases base slash damage, and reduces the stamina cost of heavy attacks. Stamina management is a key factor in long combat encounters, as executing weapon skills drains your stamina bar rapidly.

| Weapon Type | Primary Stat | Best Use Case | Combat Style |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Swords** | Sword / Strength | Boss Fights & PvP | Fast slashing, high mobility, skill-based combos |
| **Melee / Fists** | Strength | Leveling & PvE | Close-quarters combat, reliable stun options |
| **Hybrid** | Sword & Fruit | Versatile Builds | Combining elemental effects with physical attacks |

When structuring your build, you must align your weapon choice with your combat goals. Swordsmen rely on fast, successive strikes and high-mobility skills to outmaneuver opponents. Meanwhile, hybrid players use weapons to cover the cooldown periods of their fruit abilities, ensuring a constant stream of damage during intense PvE boss encounters or competitive PvP duels.

<Callout type="info" title="Stamina Management Tip">
Always keep an eye on your stamina bar during weapon combos. Running out of stamina leaves you vulnerable to counter-attacks and prevents you from dodging heavy boss skills.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Upgrading & Enhancing Weapons">

Getting a weapon drop is only the beginning of your progression journey. To unlock its full potential, you must upgrade your weapons and integrate them with auxiliary systems like Haki. Upgrades increase the base damage of your slash attacks and enhance the scaling efficiency of your sword skills.

Haki integration is particularly important for late-game content. Activating Armament Haki (Busoshoku) coats your weapon in a protective aura, granting a flat damage boost and allowing you to damage elemental Devil Fruit users who are otherwise immune to physical attacks.
