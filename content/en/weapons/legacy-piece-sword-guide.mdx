<Section icon={Swords} title="Sword Combat Mechanics & Basics">

Swords represent one of the most reliable and consistent combat styles in Legacy Piece. Unlike Devil Fruits, which often require extensive energy management and have long cooldowns, swords allow players to maintain pressure on opponents through fluid light and heavy attack chains. To succeed as a swordsman, you must understand the relationship between your base stats, weapon mastery, and active skill selection.

Every sword in the game features a unique set of moves that unlock as you increase your Mastery level. Mastery is gained by actively using the weapon in combat against NPCs or bosses. As your Mastery level rises, you will unlock key binds (typically Z, X, and C keys) that unleash high-impact slash attacks, area-of-effect guard breaks, or rapid-fire thrusts.

| Combat Attribute | Gameplay Impact | Optimization Priority |
|---|---|---|
| **Base Damage** | Scales directly with points invested in the Sword stat | High priority for all builds |
| **Mastery Level** | Unlocks active skills and increases skill damage | Required to access full move sets |
| **Attack Range** | Determines the size of your weapon hitboxes | Critical for spacing in PvP fights |
| **Swing Speed** | Dictates recovery frames and combo transition speed | Vital for fast-paced skirmishes |

<Callout type="tip" title="Mastery Farming Tip">
To level up your sword mastery quickly, locate high-health target dummies or tanky boss NPCs. Using multi-hit skills against bosses yields significantly more mastery experience per minute than grinding low-level bandits.
</Callout>

  <Step num="1" title="Acquire Basic Weaponry">

  <Step num="2" title="Invest in Sword Stats">
