<Section icon={Map} title="How to Obtain the Red Mist Weapon">

Finding the **legacy piece red mist weapon** is a journey that takes players into the most dangerous territories of the sea. It is not a weapon you can simply buy from a shop; it must be earned through combat. The primary source of this weapon is a rare drop from the "Mist Warden" boss, located on the Hidden Fog Island. 

Players should prepare for a difficult encounter, as the boss utilizes the weapon's own moveset against you. It is recommended to be at least Level 450 before attempting this farm, as the Warden has high health regeneration and can easily one-shot under-leveled pirates.

  <Step num="1" title="Locate Hidden Fog Island">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Defeat the Mist Warden">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Check Drop Rewards">

  <Step num="4" title="Verify Inventory">

  </Step>

The skills are mapped to the Z, X, and C keys by default. Each skill scales directly with your Sword and Strength stats, though Sword is the primary modifier for the Red Mist.
