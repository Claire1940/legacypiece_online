<Section icon={Swords} title="Weapon Abilities and Mastery">

Weapons are not just stat sticks; they feature unique ability sets that unlock as you increase your Weapon Mastery level. Mastery is gained by land-hitting enemies and securing final blows using the weapon. 

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Equip and Use">
    Equip your chosen weapon and use basic left-click attacks (M1s) to defeat enemies close to your level.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unlock Base Skills">
    Reach Mastery Level 25 to unlock your first active skill (usually bound to the Z key).
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unlock Advanced Skills">
    Continue training to Mastery Level 75+ to unlock high-damage combo finishers (bound to X and C keys).
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Synergize with Haki">
    Activate Armament Haki to coat your weapon, increasing base damage and bypassing elemental defenses.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Different weapons scale with different stats. Swords scale directly with your Sword stat, while weapons like the Iron Mace may scale with Strength or specialized weapon profiles.

| Build Type | Primary Stat | Recommended Weapon | Best Fruit Synergy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Pure Swordsman** | Sword (Max) | Dark Blade / Bisento | Spin / Chop (Defense) |
| **Hybrid Fighter** | Sword / Fruit | Kiribachi / Soul Cane | Light / Magma |
| **Tank Bruiser** | Defense / Strength | Iron Mace | Quake / Diamond |

<Callout type="info" title="Mastery Retention">
Weapon Mastery is permanent. If you switch to a different weapon and later return to your previous one, your unlocked skills and mastery level remain intact.
</Callout>
