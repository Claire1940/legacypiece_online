<Section icon={Map} title="Requirements & NPC Location">

To begin your journey for the **legacy piece chihiro weapon**, you must travel to the newly added **Ruin City** island. This high-level zone is home to both the Chihiro quest giver and the boss spawn point.

The acquisition process is split into two distinct quest phases, each requiring different resources, player ranks, and currency.

### Quest Stage Requirements

| Stage | Beli Cost | Rank Requirement | Required Crafting Materials |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Stage 1** | 5,000,000 Beli | None | **3x Blade of Vengeance** (Shards) |
| **Stage 2** | 10,000,000 Beli | **SS Rank or higher** | **1x Koi Soul**, **45x Tuko Nenten**, **75x Spirit Seal Ingot** |

<Callout type="warning" title="Rank Warning">

Follow these exact steps to complete the quests and forge your legendary sword.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate the Chihiro NPC">

  <Step num="2" title="Farm the Chihiro Boss">
    Locate the **Chihiro Boss** nearby in Ruin City. You can wait for his natural spawn timer or summon him manually using boss tickets. Defeat him repeatedly to collect **3x Blades of Vengeance** (also known as blade shards).
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Initiate Stage 2">
    Return to the Chihiro NPC and hand in the shards. Pay the next **10 million Beli** fee. Ensure your character profile shows **Rank SS or higher** to successfully unlock the final crafting checklist.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Gather the Final Materials">
    Defeat Ruin City mobs and bosses to secure **1x Koi Soul**, **45x Tuko Nenten**, and **75x Spirit Seal Ingots**. Once gathered, speak to the NPC one last time to receive the base Chihiro weapon!
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="How to Unlock the Secret Enten Move">

Once you obtain the sword, you will notice that the ultimate skill, **Enten**, is locked by default. To unleash the true potential of the **legacy piece chihiro weapon**, you must enchant it with a specific passive ability.

### Unlocking Kuro Instinct

To use Enten, you must roll for a specific passive called **Kuro Instinct**.

1. Travel to the **Enchantment NPC**.
2. Spend your enchantment resources to roll for weapon passives.
3. Keep rolling until you successfully secure the **Kuro Instinct** passive.
4. Equipping this passive instantly unlocks the **Enten** ability on your Chihiro hotbar, introducing giant glowing energy fish and massive slash multipliers.
