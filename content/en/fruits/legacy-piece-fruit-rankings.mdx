<Section icon={Shield} title="B-Tier Fruits and Beginner Utility">

B-Tier fruits are highly valuable for players who are still leveling up or learning basic combat mechanics. These fruits generally require less mastery to unlock their full skill sets and offer straightforward utility.

Ice and Fire are classical elemental fruits that provide steady, reliable damage. Ice is particularly useful for beginners because its freezing attacks can disable aggressive NPCs, allowing players to heal or reposition. Fire, on the other hand, deals consistent damage over time through burn effects, making it a balanced option for early quests. Dark fruit offers unique crowd control utilities, such as attracting enemies toward a central point, which synergizes well with sword-based fighting styles.

| Fruit | Best Use Case | Control Type | Ease of Use |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Ice** | Safe leveling | Freeze stun | Very High |
| **Fire** | Group clearing | Burn damage | High |
| **Dark** | Sword synergy | Pull/Gravity | Medium |

<Callout type="info" title="Starter Tip">
Do not discard a B-Tier fruit immediately if you obtain one early. They are highly efficient for clearing starter islands and saving up currency for better gear.
</Callout>
