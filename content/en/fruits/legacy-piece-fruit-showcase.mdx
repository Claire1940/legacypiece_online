<Section icon={Map} title="How to Obtain Fruits in Legacy Piece">

Securing a powerful fruit requires patience and a solid understanding of the game's acquisition systems. Players can choose between exploring the map to find natural spawns, purchasing directly from the dealer, or participating in challenging boss encounters that drop rare rewards.

Following a structured approach to fruit hunting saves time and resources, allowing you to avoid unnecessary risk in high-level zones.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate Map Spawns">
    Explore under trees across various islands. Fruits have a chance to spawn naturally every hour, but they disappear quickly if other players find them first.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Check the Fruit Dealer">
    Visit the Starter Island regularly to check the Fruit Dealer's rotating stock. You can purchase fruits using in-game Beli or premium Robux currency.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Defeat World Bosses">
    Participate in high-level boss raids. Certain bosses have a small probability of dropping rare chests containing random Devil Fruits upon defeat.
  </Step>
</Steps>
