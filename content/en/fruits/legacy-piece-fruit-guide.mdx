<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Understanding the Legacy Piece Fruit System">

In the world of Legacy Piece, fruits are the primary source of supernatural power. Inspired by the legendary One Piece universe, these fruits grant players unique elemental, transformative, or utility-based powers in exchange for the inability to swim. Understanding which fruit to consume is the most significant decision you will make in your pirate journey.

The fruit system is divided into rarities, which generally dictate the drop rate and the complexity of the moveset. However, rarity does not always equal effectiveness in every scenario. Some "Common" or "Rare" fruits provide better utility for early-game leveling than "Legendary" fruits that require high mastery levels to unlock their full potential.

| Fruit Rarity | Estimated Drop Rate | General Power Level | Recommended Use |

| **Rare** | 20% - 25% | Mid-tier damage | Mid-game progression / Specialized builds |

| **Legendary** | 1% - 3% | Elite performance | End-game meta / Competitive PvP |

<Section icon={Swords} title="In-Depth Ability Analysis for Top Fruits">

To truly master the **legacy piece fruit guide**, you must understand the individual movesets. Each fruit offers 4-5 active abilities and usually one passive or movement skill.

### 1. Light Fruit (Pika Pika no Mi)
The Light fruit is the gold standard for efficiency. Its primary movement skill allows for near-instant travel across the ocean. In combat, it relies on high-speed kicks and light beams.
- **Mirror Kick**: A fast teleport-style attack that deals heavy knockback.
- **Light Jewels**: A rain of projectiles that covers a wide area, perfect for farming NPCs.
- **Passive**: Logia immunity against non-Haki users.

### 2. Dragon Fruit
This is the ultimate "tank" fruit. Upon transformation, the player gains a massive boost to Defense and Attack Power.
- **Dragon Breath**: A concentrated beam of fire that melts boss health bars.
- **Demolition**: A ground-slam ability that stuns all nearby enemies.
- **Passive**: Enhanced natural armor and flight in dragon form.

| Ability Metric | Light Fruit | Dragon Fruit | Mochi Fruit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Range** | Long | Medium | Medium/Short |
| **Stun Duration** | Low | High | Very High |
| **Mobility** | Elite | High | Medium |
| **Skill Ceiling** | Moderate | Low | High |

<Callout type="warning" title="Mastery Requirement">
Higher-tier fruits like Dragon and Mochi require significantly more **Mastery XP** to unlock all moves. You may find yourself stuck with only one move for several levels if you don't focus on training your fruit stats early.
</Callout>

### 3. Mochi Fruit
Mochi is the favorite for PvP enthusiasts. Its abilities allow you to "stick" to opponents, making it nearly impossible for them to escape your combos. The **Power Core Abilities** associated with Mochi focus on high-speed pummeling and donut-shaped traps.

</Section>

<Section icon={Map} title="How to Obtain Fruits: Step-by-Step Guide">

Getting your hands on a high-tier fruit requires either luck or extreme dedication. There are three primary ways to acquire fruits in Legacy Piece.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Fruit Dealer (Gacha)">
    Visit the Fruit Dealer NPC in the main hub. You can spend in-game currency (Beli) or premium currency (Robux) to "spin" for a random fruit. The stock rotates every few hours, so check back often.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Map Exploration (Tree Spawns)">
    Fruits spawn randomly under trees across the various islands every 60 minutes. They despawn after 20 minutes if not picked up. Using a "Fruit Notifier" game pass is highly recommended if you choose this path.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Boss Drops and Raids">
    Certain high-level bosses have a small percentage chance to drop a Fruit Chest upon defeat. This is the most reliable way for end-game players to farm Legendary fruits while also gaining XP and Beli.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Spawn Mechanics">
In 2026, the spawn rates are slightly boosted during weekend events. If you are a free-to-play player, save your exploration time for Saturdays and Sundays to maximize your chances of finding an Epic or Legendary fruit in the wild.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Optimization: Building Around Your Fruit">

A fruit is only as good as the stats supporting it. In Legacy Piece, you have limited stat points to distribute between Strength, Defense, Sword, and Fruit.

- **Pure Fruit Build**: Put 70% of points into Fruit and 30% into Defense. This maximizes your ability damage but makes your basic M1 attacks weak.
- **Hybrid Build**: A 50/50 split between Sword and Fruit. This is ideal for fruits like **Ice** or **Dark** that provide stuns, allowing you to follow up with powerful sword skills.
