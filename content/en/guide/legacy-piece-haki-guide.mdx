<Section icon={Swords} title="Armament Haki Location and Unlock Steps">

Armament Haki (also known as Busoshoku Haki) coats your limbs or weapons in a dark, protective armor. This coating increases your damage output and allows your physical attacks to damage players or NPCs who use Logia-type Devil Fruits.

To obtain Armament Haki, you must seek out the proper trainer NPC hidden in the world. You must meet the minimum level requirement and have enough Beli in your inventory before the trainer will offer to teach you the skill.

| Requirement | Value | Details |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Minimum Level** | Level 80 | Trainer will ignore you if you are below this level |
| **Beli Cost** | 25,000 Beli | Non-refundable training fee |
| **Trainer NPC** | Haki Trainer | Located on Alabasta Island |
| **Key Benefit** | +10% Damage | Applies to basic combat, swords, and select skills |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Sail to Alabasta Island">
    Prepare your ship and navigate to Alabasta Island. Ensure you are at least Level 80 to prevent wastage of travel time.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate the Haki Trainer NPC">
    Search the desert outskirts of the main town. The trainer stands near a rocky formation away from the primary quest givers.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Pay the Training Fee">
    Interact with the NPC, accept the dialogue prompt, and pay the 25,000 Beli fee to unlock the basic Armament Haki skill.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Equip and Activate">
    Open your mastery menu or use the designated keybind to activate the Haki. Your hands or weapon will turn black, indicating it is active.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Energy Management">
Using Armament Haki drains your energy pool over time. Keep an eye on your energy bar during prolonged fights, and deactivate Haki when traveling between quest objectives to allow your pool to regenerate.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Observation Haki Location and Dodging Mechanics">

Observation Haki (Kenbunshoku Haki) grants you heightened sensory perception. When active, it allows your character to automatically dodge a set number of incoming attacks and highlights nearby enemies through walls or obstacles.

This ability is indispensable for dodging high-damage boss skills that could otherwise defeat you in a single combo. Like Armament Haki, it must be purchased from a specific trainer once you meet the strict progression milestones.

| Metric | Detail | Impact on Gameplay |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Required Level** | Level 150 | Mid-to-late game requirement |
| **Cost** | 50,000 Beli | Double the cost of Armament Haki |
| **Trainer NPC** | Mentalist | Located on Skypiea (Angel Island) |
| **Base Dodges** | 3 Dodges | Can be increased through mastery training |

<Callout type="info" title="Dodge Cooldowns">
Every time you auto-dodge an attack, a charge is consumed. Once all charges are depleted, your character will take full damage until the dodge meter regenerates. Avoid relying solely on auto-dodge; manual movement is still necessary.
</Callout>

To train your Observation Haki and increase the maximum number of dodges, you must keep the ability active while taking damage from enemies. Over time, your dodge capacity will scale up to a maximum of 8 dodges, significantly improving your survivability.

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Conqueror's Haki and Mastery Leveling">

Conqueror's Haki (Haoshoku Haki) is the rarest form of Haki in the game. Unlike the other two types, it cannot be bought from a standard trainer NPC. Instead, it is tied to character generation or special items, representing the user's inner willpower.

When activated, Conqueror's Haki releases a powerful shockwave that temporarily stuns all low-level NPCs and players within a wide radius. This is incredibly useful for clearing groups of enemies during farming runs or interrupting enemy casting animations in PvP.
