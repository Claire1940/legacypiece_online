S-Tier fruits represent the peak of performance in the current meta. **Light** is highly favored by players who value rapid map traversal and long-range combat options, making it one of the most efficient tools for clearing quests. **Dragon** stands out due to its massive area-of-effect damage and raw power, although it requires a significant investment of resources and is generally more suited for late-game builds.

A-Tier fruits offer excellent performance and can comfortably carry players through all available content. **Mochi** excels in creating fluid offensive combinations, while **Quake** offers strong crowd control capabilities to manage groups of enemies. **Magma** remains a highly reliable choice for players focusing on consistent damage during progression, offering a great balance of power and ease of use.

B-Tier fruits are solid options, particularly for beginners or players who prefer specific utility setups. **Ice** and **Fire** provide straightforward, elemental combat options that are easy to learn. **Dark** offers unique crowd control utility, which can be useful when paired with specific weapon builds or during group activities.
