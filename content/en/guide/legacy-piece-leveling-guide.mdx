<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Leveling Strategy">

Maximizing your XP gain requires a systematic approach to every island you visit. Follow this step-by-step loop to clear zones efficiently.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Accept All Local Quests">
    Locate the quest givers near the docks or main town. Always stack multiple quests if they target the same enemy types to double your XP yield per kill.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Group and Aggro Enemies">
    Do not fight enemies one by one. Run past a group of 3 to 5 targets to draw their aggro, pull them into a tight cluster, and unleash your AoE skills.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Prioritize Elite Targets">
    Once your standard quest goals are complete, target local mini-bosses or elite units. They offer significantly higher XP chunks and valuable gear drops.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Upgrade Gear and Move On">
    Check local vendors or drop tables for weapon upgrades before leaving an island. Once you reach the maximum recommended level for the zone, set sail immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Grouping Tip">
When grouping mobs, use basic ranged attacks or block-dashes to pull distant archers or gunners into the melee group for a clean sweep.
</Callout>
