<Section icon={Map} title="Early Game Farming Routes (Level 1-50)">

For new players, the **legacy piece money farm guide** starts at the very first island. The key to early wealth is not just fighting, but completing quests efficiently. The early progression follows a linear path through the starter islands. Your goal is to reach a level where you can "one-shot" mobs, allowing you to clear quest objectives in under a minute.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Bandit Loop">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Shells Island Efficiency">

  <Step num="3" title="Orange Town Chest Run">
